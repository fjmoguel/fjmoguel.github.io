About Me

A proud Boston University alumni who majored in Computer Science (Machine Learning and Theoretical CS focus) with a minor in Business Administration, graduated May 2025. Currently a University of Texas McCombs School of Business M.S Student in IT & Management. Originally from Mexico, I’m passionate about machine learning, finance, and using analytics to solve real-world challenges. I’m fluent in English and Spanish, an IB diploma holder, and enjoy sports, guitar, bird photography, skiing, and trail running. My Labrador, Viena, is my trail companion, and I bring the same endurance and curiosity from the trail into every project I take on.
