About Me

A proud Boston University alum who majored in Computer Science (Machine Learning and Theoretical CS focus) with a minor in Business Administration, graduated May 2025. Currently a University of Texas McCombs School of Business M.S Student in IT & Management. I’ve completed marathons in London, NYC, Boston, Chicago and Tokyo, and ran the entire MBTA subway system (130 km) to raise awareness for mental health. Originally from Mexico, I’m passionate about machine learning, finance, and using analytics to solve real-world challenges. I’m fluent in English and Spanish, an IB diploma holder, and enjoy sports, guitar, bird photography, skiing, and trail running. My Labrador, Viena, is my trail companion, and I bring the same endurance and curiosity from the trail into every project I take on.
