Assignment 1: Data Collection and Analysis

This assignment focused on collecting and analyzing elevator arrival data to determine the optimal location to wait in the CDS lobby, minimizing the expected walking distance to the next arriving elevator. I implemented solutions by calculating the average inter-arrival times of the elevators, determining probabilities based on these times, and using weighted averages to identify the best waiting position.
